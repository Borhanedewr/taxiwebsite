About us – Your Taxi Team in Neumünster

Our Story

TaxiGo Neumünster was founded in 2025 with a clear vision: to revolutionize taxi service in Neumünster through modern technology, sustainable transport, and excellent customer service.

What started as a small idea quickly developed into a leading taxi company in the region. Our founder, an experienced taxi driver with over 15 years of experience, recognized the need for a reliable, transparent, and customer-oriented taxi service in Neumünster.

From the very beginning, we have relied on eco-friendly vehicles and digital booking systems to best meet the needs of our customers while minimizing our ecological footprint.

Our Mission

Safety

We prioritize the safety of our passengers through regular maintenance of our vehicles, comprehensive driver training, and strict quality controls.

Sustainability

With our hybrid and electric fleet, we focus on environmentally friendly mobility and make an active contribution to climate protection in our city.

Reliability

We understand how important punctuality is for our customers. Therefore, reliability is a central value in our daily actions.
